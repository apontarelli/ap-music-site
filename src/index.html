i'm antonio

I sing, play violin & keys, and sometimes mess around on the mandolin. Writing songs is my way of staying happy and making sense of this crazy world. I was a classical musician until my dad turned me onto Bill Withers and Earth Wind & Fire. I've done weird - but fun - stuff along the way like auto-tuning dog barks to Katy Perry songs and performing at Carnegie Hall, but here I am now playing pop / funk music with some of the kindest and most talented musicians I know.
